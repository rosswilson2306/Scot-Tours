Regions

Scot Tours has trips avaiable across all regions of the country. The city tours offer the chance to see different regions of the country in one trip, travelling between cities like Ediburgh and Inverness via train.

Some of the tour options stick to specific regions, like the Hiking tours which are located across the Cairngorms, Grampian mountains and other Highland spots like the famous Glen Coe.

Many of our coastal tours are situated amongst the Hebridean Isles. You will have the chance to take ferry journeys between islands like Skye, Jura, Isla, Arran and many more.

Team Members

Douglas McKenzie

Tour Coordinator

Douglas heads up the team that organises all of the tours. As well as taking bookings and organising accomodation, Douglas and his team are constantly scouting new and exciting locations for tours across our beautiful country.

Sally Gillespie

Tour Leader

Sally leads our excellent selection of tour guides. As locals of the regions they cover the tour guides have expert local knowledge of their areas.

Stuart Allison

History Buff

Our expert of the history of Scotland is Stuart Allison. He has been a curator of Scotland's history for more than 50 years. Stuart was professor of Scottish studies at Edinburgh University for 25 years and joined Scot Tours in 2015.

Margaret Kerr

Wildlife Expert

Margaret is our wildlife expert. She studied as a vet but decided to move in a different direction. She oversees the training of the tour guides, making sure they have an intricate knowledge of the wildlife of Scotland.
